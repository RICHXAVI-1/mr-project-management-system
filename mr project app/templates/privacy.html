1. Scope & Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data collected through the Services and during interactions with Mr. Project, including account registration, purchases, support requests, and communications. It does not apply to information collected offline or by third parties (unless otherwise stated).

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information automatically collected when you use the Services.

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, billing address.
  • Account information: username, password (hashed), profile details.
  • Project materials: documents, thesis drafts, datasets, research files, briefings, and other materials you upload to receive services.
  • Payment information: billing and payment details — note we use third-party payment processors and do not store full payment card details on our servers unless explicitly stated.
  • Communications: messages, support requests, and feedback you send to us.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, time spent on pages, and other analytics.
  • Device & technical data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, and network information.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: information collected via cookies, web beacons, and local storage (see Section 6).

2.3 Information From Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party services (for example, payment processors, identity verification providers, or analytics platforms). We rely on such sources only as necessary to provide and secure the Services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: to provide, manage, and improve the Services, process orders, and deliver project deliverables.
  • Communication: to respond to inquiries, send confirmations, invoices, updates, and customer support messages.
  • Payments & billing: to process payments, prevent fraud, and manage billing.
  • Security & compliance: to detect and prevent abuse, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our policies.
  • Personalization & analytics: to analyze usage, improve features, and tailor user experience.
  • Marketing (where permitted): to send newsletters or promotional materials if you have opted in. You may opt out at any time.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable law requires us to identify a lawful basis to process personal data, we rely on one or more of the following:

  • Performance of a contract: processing necessary to provide Services you requested.
  • Legal obligation: compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Legitimate interests: for fraud prevention, troubleshooting, analytics, and improving our Services (we balance these interests against your privacy rights).
  • Consent: where you have given clear consent (for example, marketing communications or certain cookies).

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We may disclose personal data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics providers). We require such vendors to use your data only to perform services for us and to protect it appropriately.
  • Legal obligations: when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of rights: to enforce our Terms of Service, protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of company assets; you will be notified where required by law.

We will not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide, secure, and improve the Services. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings

You may control cookies through your browser settings and other tools. Blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Services. For more details about the specific cookies we use and how to manage them, see our Cookie Notice (if available) or contact us at the address below.

7. Security of Your Data

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These measures include encrypted communications (TLS/HTTPS), access controls, and security monitoring.

However, no internet or electronic system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and are not liable for unauthorized access beyond our reasonable control. If we become aware of a data breach that creates a risk of harm, we will notify affected individuals and regulators as required by law.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When personal data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you

To exercise your rights or submit a request, please contact us using the details in Section 13. We may need to verify your identity before responding to requests and may limit requests that are manifestly unfounded or excessive under applicable law.
